# Post Type Specs

## metric

Report a measured change with context and caveats.

## lesson

Explain what changed in judgment or practice.

## deep-dive

Break down a decision, system, mistake, or result.

## bts

Show behind-the-scenes work without manufacturing drama.

## hot-take

Make a pointed claim backed by concrete reasoning.

## recap

Summarize recent progress and next steps.

## launch

Announce a shipped thing and why it matters.

## failure

Explain what failed, what was learned, and what changes.
